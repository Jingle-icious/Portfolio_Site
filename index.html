About Me

I am a Junior UX Designer at LMI, currently dedicated to the RAPTR® project. My expertise lies at the intersection of complex data visualization and mission-critical operations within the Space Market.

My journey into UX began during high school while interning for MITRE. Working on space-related projects there sparked a deep interest in how design can make specialized, high-stakes data accessible and functional.

I pursued an accelerated path at the University of Colorado Boulder, earning my B.S. in Creative Technology & Design in just three years. During this time, I secured an internship at LMI the summer after my freshman year, maintaining the role throughout my junior and senior years until graduating in May 2025 and transitioning to full-time.
